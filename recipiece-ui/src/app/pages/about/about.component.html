All about Recipiece

User Agreement Billing
Recipiece does not store any billing information on our own servers; rather, our billing vendor is responsible for securely maintaining your data.

Recipiece will only bill you if your sign up for the premium tier. Should you encounter any issues or wish to dispute your charges, please reach out to us at support@recipiece.org.

Failure to pay when subscribed to the premium tier will result in loss off access to that tier and associated data.
Open Source
Recipiece is built on Angular and Python, and is available on Github under the GNU Affero General Public License v3.0. Bugs may be filed in the issue tracker on Github.

If you're particularly interested in contributing, feel free to make a pull request on the repo. We'd love to repay you with a free subscription to Recipiece, if your contribution is accepted.

Recipiece uses the USDA's FoodData Central API to help categorize your shopping list items. No personal or identifying information is shared with this service.
